Scale & Partitioning Strategy:   CELL: ~10⁹ rows, BIGINT PK, partitioned by cell_id % 1024  |  MEASUREMENT: ~4×10⁹ rows (4 modalities/cell), composite PK, Parquet columnar ~10:1 compression  |  EMBEDDING: ~10⁹ rows (384-dim), pgvector ANN search  |  FEATURE_GRAPH: ~10⁶ edges, in-memory during training  |  Same-cell join: cell_id is the universal anchor — all modalities share one cell_id per physical cell
